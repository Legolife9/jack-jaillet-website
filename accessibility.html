Accessibility

This site should work for everyone. Here is where it stands and how to reach me if something is in your way.

My commitment

I want this website to be usable by as many people as possible, regardless of ability or the technology they use. This site targets conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

What's in place

The site is built with semantic HTML landmarks and a logical heading structure, and every page includes a skip-to-content link. All functionality works with a keyboard, with clearly visible focus indicators. Text and background colors meet or exceed the WCAG AA 4.5:1 contrast ratio in both the light and dark themes, and the dark theme follows your system preference automatically. Animations are subtle, never carry required information, and are disabled for visitors who have turned on reduced motion in their system settings. Images have descriptive alternative text, links that open a new tab say so to screen readers, and the resume page prints cleanly for offline use.

Known limitations

Contact currently relies on an email link rather than an on-page form. If email is not workable for you, you can also reach me through LinkedIn (opens in new tab).

Found a barrier?

If any part of this site is difficult for you to use, I genuinely want to know. Email me at contact@jackjaillet.com and describe the problem and the page it happened on. I will do my best to fix it promptly and get back to you.

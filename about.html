Hey, thanks for
stopping by.

I'm Jack (he/him), an 18-year-old business student in Massachusetts with a constant drive to learn, serve, and lead. I graduated from Montachusett Regional Vocational Technical School in 2026, where I studied Business Technology, served as Class Treasurer and Class Secretary, and was a member of the National Honor Society.

This fall, I'm starting at UMass Amherst's Isenberg School of Management. My major is undeclared for now, and I could not be more excited about the path ahead.

Outside the classroom you'll usually find me behind a camera, planning a service project, or training people to step in when it matters. I founded Charming Memories Photography, lead multimedia for the MassSTAR youth leadership conference, and was named the 2024-25 Massachusetts Leo of the Year by Lions Clubs International.

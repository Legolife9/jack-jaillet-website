July 2026

Three years of MassSTAR

Three years ago, I walked into MassSTAR as a sophomore delegate with no idea how much this conference would shape who I am today. In 2024, I came as a delegate, soaking everything in. In 2025 and 2026, I came back as part of the Conference Planning Committee as Multimedia Director, taking on a challenge I didn't fully understand until I was in it: capture every meaningful moment from Friday through Sunday, edit on the fly, and deliver a finished video Sunday afternoon that includes content from that same day.

Over these three years, I've grown in ways I didn't expect. My confidence in my own voice, my ability to lead, and the connections I've built with people across Massachusetts have all become things I carry with me beyond the conference walls. MassSTAR is put on by the Massachusetts Youth Leadership Foundation and is 100% volunteer run, from planning to production.

June 2026

Closing the high school chapter

A few weeks ago, I closed one of the most meaningful chapters of my life and walked across the Montachusett Regional Vocational Technical School District graduation stage. High school gave me so much more than a diploma. I had the privilege of serving as Class Treasurer, Secretary, and member of the National Honor Society, Leo of the Year for my school, and then Leo of the Year for the state of Massachusetts.

None of these things were done alone. I met so many fabulous people along the way, teachers, mentors, friends, and community members, who believed in me, challenged me, and showed up when it mattered. Now I am heading into the next chapter with purpose and the ambition to serve, do good, and make the people who believed in me proud.
